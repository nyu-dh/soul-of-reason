Welcome! "Raising the Volume" is a cross-departmental NYU initiative to transform Soul of Reason, a historically rich 1970s NYC radio program on Black and Latinx culture, into a collaboratively curated collection dataset.

Our project team hosts community events—including Transcribe-a-thons and Wikidata Edit-a-thons—and supports student-led scholarship to enrich, analyze, and share the collection in new and newly accessible ways.
